# Fitness & Wellness Management System

A custom Frappe application built on ERPNext v15+ covering:
- Member & Subscription Management
- Class & Schedule Management
- Trainer & Staff Management
- Facility & Equipment Management
- Health & Fitness Assessment
- Nutrition & Diet Management
- Billing & Finance

## Installation

```bash
bench new-app fitness_wellness
bench --site your-site.local install-app fitness_wellness
bench --site your-site.local migrate
bench restart
```
